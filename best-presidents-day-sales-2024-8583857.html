Ugg, Lululemon, J.Crew, and More

June 15, 2024
blog

To whoever decided that Presidents Day weekend would be an unofficial shopping holiday, thank you. Because right now, for no reason other than to make an already good three-day weekend even better, some of our favorite brands and retailers have marked down thousands of items.

While there are hundreds of sales to choose from this weekend, there are four I won’t be missing. At Lululemon, must-have athletic apparel is up to 54 percent off, while classic spring pieces are 30 percent off at J.Crew. During Nordstrom’s Presidents Day sale you’ll find thousands of deals, including discounts on Ugg and Tory Burch, while at Amazon, a secret denim sale includes $19 jeans.

Shop four of the best deals from each retailer’s Presidents Day Sale below, with prices starting at just $19.

54% Off: Lululemon Tapered-Leg Mid-Rise Luxtreme Pant

Lululemon We Made Too Much

Lululemon has added a number of new styles to its We Made Too Much section, where you can score major savings on some of the brand’s best-selling items. One of the best deals is on the everyday Tapered-Leg Mid-Rise Luxtreme Pant, which is now up to 54 percent off. But if you’re looking for something a little more workout-ready, consider grabbing the famous Align leggings — loved by more than 8,000 shoppers who have called the style “buttery soft” and “supportive without feeling compressive” — while prices start at just $69.

There are also a number of bras on sale, so no matter your style of workout, you’re covered. The now-$39 SmoothCover bra is perfect for yoga, offering light support, removable cups, and the brand’s four-way stretch material. But if you’re needing something that can hold up to more high-intensity workouts, grab the Energy Bra. According to 36DD-shopper who’s always had to layer sports bras, “This is the first time [they've] ever been able to run in a singular bra.” “This bra is *truly* high support,” they said.

30% Off: J.Crew Emilie Sweater Lady Jacket

J.Crew Pre-Spring Sale

If you’re looking to give your closet a refresh ahead of spring, there are few better places to start than J.Crew, which just launched its Pre-Spring Sale. Right now, a number of just-added styles and best-sellers are 30 percent off with the code SHOPNOW, including the fan-favorite Emilie Sweater Lady Jacket, which features chic gold buttons and nautical stripes — so yes, I’ll be wearing this now and on chilly summer nights. Another staple I’m looking to grab is the Gwyneth Slip Skirt; available in eight colors, it boasts more than 200 five-star ratings, is elegant, easy to style, and now just $63.

I’m using this as an opportunity to grab wardrobe staples for less, so I’m of course also eyeing the brand’s Demi-Boot Crop Jean, which shoppers have said “fit perfectly and are super flattering,” and look good with everything, including flats, boots, and heels. I also love these now-$83 sneakers, which boast a classic silhouette and eye-catching metallic stripe.

55% Off: Cole Haan Lux Pinch Penny Loafer

Nordstrom Presidents Day Sale

At Nordstrom, thousands of items are on sale, including major savings on brands I love. Cole Haan’s sleek Lux Pinch Penny Loafers are 55 percent off, and given that the style is nearly identical to ones Katie Holmes and Gigi Hadid recently wore, I’m immediately adding them to my cart. Another great shoe deal is on these flats from Sam Edelman, which are “extremely comfortable,” according to shoppers, thanks to insoles that make it so “you don’t even feel like you have shoes on.”

You’ll also find shockingly good deals on more luxe brands including Tory Burch and Ugg. This elegant Tory Buch bag, which is available in a trending chili red color and features a removable chain strap, is 30 percent off, while Ugg’s Joselyn Lounge Cardigan is now $83.  

$19 Denim Steal: Gloria Vanderbilt Amanda Classic High-Rise Tapered Jeans

Amazon

I recently discovered Amazon’s secret denim sale, and the deals are so good, I kind of can’t keep quiet about it. Gloria Vanderbilt’s tapered jeans are now just $19, while Levi's iconic Ribcage Straight Ankle Jeans are 60 percent off. According to fans of the Levi’s style, the straight-leg jeans “offer a flattering fit, quality craftsmanship, and versatility that make them a valuable wardrobe staple.” Plus, “they make your butt look great.”

Other great deals include Lee’s now-$20 relaxed-fit jeans, which have more than 16,000 fans on Amazon, as well as Ettelo’s 30-percent off straight-leg style.
