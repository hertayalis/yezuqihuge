Tony Bellew, born Anthony Lewis Bellew on November 30th, 1982 in England, is a former professional boxer.

He discovered his passion for boxing at an early age and remained steadfast throughout his career, climbing to the top of the sport’s popularity until he eventually retired.

Tony Bellew, a three-time ABA heavyweight champion, made his pro boxing debut in 2007 and won a decisive second-round victory against journeyman Jamie Ambler.

Some of the fights he was in throughout his active career years are as follows: Bellew vs. Cleverly, Bellew vs. Chilemba, Bellew vs. Stevenson, Bellew vs. Masternak, Bellew vs. Makabu, Bellew vs. Flores, Bellew vs. Haye, and Bellew vs. Usyk.

Tony Bellew had a career that extended over a decade, beginning in 2007 and ending in 2018 when he was defeated by Usyk. There were 30 victories out of a possible 34 professional fights before he called it quits.
