Megan Fox Is a Forest Nymph in a Green Bikini While Climbing Up a Tree

August 30, 2024
blog

Megan Fox is showing off her love of nature in a steamy new photo shoot.

The Till Death star made waves on Instagram over the weekend when she shared snaps of herself climbing up a tree branch while wearing a barely-there lime-green string bikini. She accessorized the swimsuit sparingly, opting to simply wear headpieces such as a purple butterfly and long white hair extensions.

“The forest is my oldest friend,” she captioned the pictures, taken by Cibelle Levi.

Fiancé Machine Gun Kelly voiced his praise in the comments, writing, “If this is what a wild animal looks like, i'd let it maul me.”

Lately, the actor has maintained a relatively quiet presence on social media over the past few months amid rumors of a rift in her relationship with Kelly. The couple reportedly broke up in February, following a now-deleted Instagram post in which Fox cryptically hinted at a betrayal by quoting lyrics from Beyoncé’s Lemonade single “Pray You Catch Me.” She later denied infidelity rumors in another post, writing, “There has been no third-party interference in this relationship of any kind.”

Though details of the split are still largely unknown, the two have publicly reconciled, with Fox and Kelly once again attending red-carpet events together and jetting out for a romantic getaway in Hawaii.

Fox returned to Instagram by posting another since-deleted bikini photo in June. At the time, the snaps captured the actor wearing a stringy black two-piece with a strapless cutout top and thong bottoms. She upped the ante with her accessories, which included several gold bangles and bracelets.
