As a coach, Williams was an assistant coach from 1992-95 for Norview High School, where he also coached track and field.

He returned to William & Mary as a coach in 1996, coaching running backs from 1996–97 and defensive backs from 1998–2000.

Williams got his first NFL coaching experience coaching under Tony Dungy in the Tampa Bay Buccaneers organization in 2001

With the Colts, he won Super Bowl XLI against the Chicago Bears. In January 2012, he was hired to be the defensive coordinator of the Minnesota Vikings.

In January 2014, Williams joined the Detroit Lions defensive coaching staff and in February 2018, he returned to the Indianapolis Colts as the defensive backs/safeties coach.

On February 2, 2022, Williams became the defensive coordinator for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League.
