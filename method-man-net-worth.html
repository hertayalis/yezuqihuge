Early Life and Education

Method Man was born on March 2, 1971, in Hempstead, New York. He grew up in Staten Island, where he attended the now-closed Tottenville High School.

After high school, Method Man briefly attended community college before dropping out to pursue a music career. He formed a rap group with his friend RZA and eventually caught the attention of Ol’ Dirty Bastard, who introduced him to the rest of the members of the Wu-Tang Clan.
