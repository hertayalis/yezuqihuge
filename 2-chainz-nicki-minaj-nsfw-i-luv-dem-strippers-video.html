2 Chainz + Nicki Minaj Make It Rain in NSFW I Luv Dem Strippers Video

August 15, 2024
blog

2 Chainz and Nicki Minaj shower scantily clad women with stacks of singles and a whole lot of love in their new video for 'I Luv Dem Strippers,' an ode to exotic dancers that was released last week. We should note that unless you're employed by a strip joint, this is probably a bit NSFW.

The clip opens with shots of a locker room, sparkling bikini tops and stacks of dollar bills -- all strip club staples. We soon see the exotic dancers primping in the mirror, getting dressed (just to get undressed later), putting their names on their lockers and putting on heels so high they may cause nosebleeds for the rest of us.

In no time at all, the girls are hard at work doing what they do best for 2 Chainz: grinding and doing acrobatic tricks on poles and likely disappointing the fathers who likely abandoned them at a young age. He shows his appreciation with cash and by offering on lucky lady one of his chains, which she carries in her teeth.

Minaj enters midway through the video and appears to be having the most fun out of anyone. She throws cash, caresses behinds (they must be in the champagne room) and unleashes her braggadocio rhymes with a few giggles here and there.

“That song is just a natural organic song for anybody from my region,” 2 Chainz told Rap-Up TV in an interview about the track. “We’re slightly infested with strip clubs, but it’s a beautiful thing when you learn the art of it as far as the pole tricks and things like that.” It certainly looks pretty athletic, to say the least. Keep your eyes peeled for fellow Atlanta natives T.I. and Young Jeezy, who also enjoy the company of some, er, pole artists as well.
