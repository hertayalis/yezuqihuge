Discover the net worth, age, career, wife, salary, biography, and latest updates about Satnam Singh.

Satnam Singh Bhamara, a renowned professional wrestler and former basketball player, has created a stir in the world of sports with his outstanding skills and unwavering dedication. At present, he is affiliated with All Elite Wrestling (AEW), leaving an enduring legacy as the first Indian and Sikh player to be drafted into the NBA (National Basketball Association).

Profile Summary

Satnam Singh’s Early Life

Birthed on December 10, 1995, in Baloke, Punjab, India, Satnam Singh was nurtured in a supportive and caring family that fueled his passion for sports. His parents, Balbir Singh Bhamara and Sukhwinder Kaur, provided unwavering support, laying the groundwork for his extraordinary journey in the sports realm.

Satnam Singh’s Basketball Journey

Satnam Singh’s foray into basketball commenced during his high school years at the prestigious IMG Academy in Bradenton, Florida. Under the mentorship of experienced coaches and amidst top-tier talent, Singh refined his skills and displayed his potential, making a significant impact in the center position.

Satnam Singh’s Professional Basketball Path

Satnam Singh’s talent and hard work garnered well-deserved attention. In the 2015 NBA draft, he etched his name in history as the first Indian-born player chosen, picked as the 52nd overall pick by the Dallas Mavericks. This groundbreaking feat constituted a new chapter for both Satnam and Indian basketball.

Although he didn’t play in college, overseas professionally, or in the NBA Development League, Satnam Singh’s draft selection solidified his standing as an exceptional talent. His journey continued with the Mavericks, and he also played for the Texas Legends, the Mavericks’ affiliate team in the NBA G League. Singh’s dedication and contributions on the court demonstrated his potential and determination to thrive at the highest level.

Satnam Singh’s National Team Success

In addition to his success at the club level, Satnam Singh has consistently showcased his commitment to his country and the sport by representing the Indian national basketball team on numerous occasions, commencing from the national under-16 team at the 2009 FIBA Asia Under-16 Championship to his debut for the senior Indian national team at the 2011 FIBA Asia Championship.

Satnam Singh’s Wrestling Career

Satnam Singh’s ardor for sports extends beyond the basketball court. In 2017, he underwent a workout at the WWE Performance Center, unveiling his potential in the professional wrestling domain. Taking a step further in his wrestling aspirations, Singh signed with AEW All Elite Wrestling in September 2021. His debut on AEW Dynamite marked an exciting new chapter as he targeted Samoa Joe and aligned himself with Jay Lethal and Sonjay Dutt.

Satnam Singh’s Family and Siblings

Satnam Singh’s closely-knit family has been pivotal in his journey. He is the proud son of Balbir Singh Bhamara and Sukhwinder Kaur. Additionally, he receives unwavering support and encouragement from his two siblings, Sarabjot Kaur and Beant Singh.

Satnam Singh’s Educational Background

During his formative years, Satnam Singh pursued his education at IMG Academy from 2010 to 2015. This academic and athletic amalgamation equipped him with the necessary tools to excel both on and off the court, underscoring his dedication to personal growth and development.

Satnam Singh’s Wife

Who is Satnam Singh’s wife? He is in a committed relationship with his fiancée, Jyoti Banwait. Balancing his personal life with his professional aspirations, Singh remains dedicated to his wrestling and basketball career.

How tall is Satnam Singh?

Boasting an impressive height of 7 feet 2 inches (2.18 meters) and weighing 138 kilograms (304 pounds), Satnam Singh commands a formidable physical presence. His stature, combined with his remarkable athleticism, has contributed to his success on the basketball court and in the wrestling arena.

Satnam Singh’s Net Worth and Salary

Satnam Singh’s invaluable contributions to the world of sports have garnered him well-deserved recognition and financial rewards. With an approximate net worth of $5 million and an annual salary of $1.1 million, he has solidified his position as a distinguished figure in the sporting industry.
