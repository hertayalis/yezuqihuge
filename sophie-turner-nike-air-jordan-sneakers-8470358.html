Sophie Turner Has Worn These Comfy Sneakers 23 Times in the Past Year

July 28, 2024
blog

Having a few sneaker styles in your closet will serve you well, especially when pairing them with a variety of outfits. I know I’ve hit the sneaker lottery when I repeatedly wear a specific pair with almost every OOTD. And I’m not the only one to favor a comfy shoe. Celebrities like Jennifer Lawrence, Meghan Markle, and Amal Clooney all rely on tried-and-true sneaker styles to complement casual looks.   

Actress Sophie Turner is also no stranger to incorporating comfortable footwear into her street style. She’s been spotted in London on several strolls and going on walks with her children in New York City, favoring one sneaker in particular: the Nike Air Jordan 1 Low.   

Nike Air Jordan 1 Low 

The light gray and baby blue shoes that Turner has been spotted in over and over again were, unfortunately, a limited edition color that is no longer available on Nike’s website. However, the good news is that you can get your hands on the same style in other color-blocked options like gray and black, red and black, and even an all-white version.

Turner has proven the quality and longevity of the Air Jordan 1 Low sneaker that numerous shoppers call out for being “so comfy” since she’s opted to wear them at least 23 times on walks since February 2023. The actress has been seen wearing them with long trench coats and jeans, puffer jackets and flared sweats, and even with gray slacks and a sparkly halter top when grabbing dinner with gal pal Taylor Swift.    

The original Air Jordan was released in collaboration with basketball legend Michael Jordan back in 1985. It has since been a coveted Nike footwear style by ‘sneakerheads’ — aka shoe collectors — and the masses who’ve found it reliable in comfort and support. Jordans are released in new styles numerous times throughout the year and often sell out quickly. 

Combining Nike Air Technology, genuine leather, and a cushiony rubber outsole, the AJ1 Low resembles the brand's Hoops Original shoe but with a lighter weight and a sleek profile that is “comfortable” from the first wear, as one shopper put it. Another person said the sneakers run “true to size” while adding that they “give the appearance of a smaller foot without sacrificing comfort.” A third reviewer loves being able to “wear them with anything.” 

Emulate Sophie Turner’s street style with a pair of Nike Air Jordan 1 Lows, and keep scrolling for other shopper-loved Jordan sneakers.
