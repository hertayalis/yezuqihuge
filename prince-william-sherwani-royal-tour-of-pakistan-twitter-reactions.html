Twitter Reacts to Prince William Wearing a Sherwani in Pakistan

August 03, 2024
blog
  • On Tuesday, the second day of their royal tour of Pakistan, Prince William and Duchess Kate attended a special reception hosted by British High Commissioner Thomas Drew at the Pakistan Monument in Islamabad, Pakistan.
  • Kate looked stunning in a shimmering, emerald-green gown by Jenny Packham and Will set Twitter abuzz when he stepped out in a dark teal traditional sherwani by Pakistani brand Naushemian for the formal event.

Prince William and Duchess Kate stepped out on Tuesday evening for a special formal reception hosted by British High Commissioner Thomas Drew at the Pakistan Monument in Islamabad.

The Duchess of Cambridge was predictably stunning in her floor-length, sparkling emerald-green Jenny Packham gown, which she paired with metallic heeled sandals and dangling earrings from O'Nitaa.

Related Story

While the Duke of Cambridge's fashion choices don't typically get much attention, his dark teal traditional sherwani (a formal coatlike piece) by Pakistani brand Naushemian has been the talk of Twitter since the couple stepped out.

The reactions have been largely positive. As one Twitter user wrote in reply to a post about the ensemble on a fan account for the duke and duchess, "This means so much to us, so many times we have seen western countries making our culture as sign of terrorism and mockery. The future king and queen of [the] UK respecting our culture means a lot."

Read more of Twitter's reactions to the look below.

Daily Mail royal correspondent Rebecca English went so far as to declare that Will's look was the highlight of the night. "Amazing Jenny Packham dress for Kate tonight — but it's all about WWW.....What William Wore!" she wrote.
