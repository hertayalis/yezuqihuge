2 Chainz | Career

2 Chainz started his music career as one half the hip hop duo “Playaz Circle” with his longtime friend Earl “Dolla Boy” Conyers.

Soon after releasing their first independent album, “United We Stand, United We Fall,” in 2002, they were signed to Ludacris’s just-formed record label Disturbing Tha Peace.

However, after 13 years and several hits later, 2 Chainz decided to go solo.

In his first year as a solo artist, he released his first studio album, “Based on a T.R.U. Story.”

2 Chainz was nominated for three Grammy Awards, including the Best Rap Album that year.

So far, he’s released six studio albums, and a seventh album titled “Dope Don’t Sell Itself” is on its way.
