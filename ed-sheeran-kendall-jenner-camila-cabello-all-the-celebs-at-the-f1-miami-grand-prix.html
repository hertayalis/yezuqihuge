Brittany and Patrick Mahomes, Ed Sheeran and Camila Cabello: All the celebs at the F1 Miami Grand Pr

July 02, 2024
blog

    The 2024 Formula One season hits Miami, Florida this weekend, and the celebrities were out in force to make it the most glamorous event of the season, including NFL star Patrick Mahomes, Ed Sheeran, Kendall Jenner, Camila Cabello, and Red, White & Royal Blue actor Taylor Zakhar Perez.

    Ed took to the Hard Rock Beach Club trackside stage on Saturday May 4, performing six songs including 'Shape of You' and 'Bad Habits', while Camila and Taylor were both spotted with Ferrari.

    Tom Brady was also seen in the garages, and Patrick was joined by wife Brittany as he checked in on his 2023 investment in the Alpine Racing F1 team.

    The Grand Prix will take place on May 5, 2024, at the Miami International Autodrome in Miami Gardens, Florida. It is the sixth race of the season,  and drivers are still battling for their starting grid positions. Max Verstappen has started all five races this season from pole position, making him the only driver this century to achieve that feat.
